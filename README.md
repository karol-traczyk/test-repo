## Polish National Holiday - August 15th

August 15th is a national holiday in Poland, celebrated for two significant reasons:

- **Assumption of Mary** (*Wniebowzięcie Najświętszej Maryi Panny*) - A major religious celebration in the Catholic tradition
- **Polish Armed Forces Day** (*Święto Wojska Polskiego*) - Commemorating the historic 1920 Battle of Warsaw

This day is an official day off in Poland, recognizing both its religious and historical importance.
