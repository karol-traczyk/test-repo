 # Finland — Quick Facts

 - Capital: Helsinki
 - Population: ~5.6 million
 - Official languages: Finnish, Swedish; Sámi recognized regionally
 - Currency: Euro (EUR)
 - Government: Parliamentary republic
 - EU: Member since 1995; Schengen since 2001; NATO member since 2023
 - Geography: ~188,000 lakes; ~75% forested; highest point Halti (1,324 m)
 - Climate: Cold winters; midnight sun and aurora borealis in the north
 - Culture: Sauna is ubiquitous (over 2 million saunas)
 - Economy: Strong tech and design; home of Nokia, Rovio, Supercell
 - Education: High-performing, equitable school system
 - Time zone: EET (UTC+2), EEST (UTC+3 in summer)
 - Country code: +358; ccTLD: .fi
 - Flag: Blue Nordic cross on white
